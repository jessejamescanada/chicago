Who I am

I have been a professional photographer for 13 years now. I started in Niagara Falls, Ontario and still shoot there in the summer. But with Chicago becoming my 2nd home, I am excited to offer family, engagement, elopement and maternity photography in Chicago and the surrounding areas.

My style is a mix of traditional portraits and photojournalistic. I do give gentle direction, but I try to keep things feeling and looking natural. I've always believed that the more comfortable my clients feel, the better their photos turn out!

With the pandemic upon us, I will be shooting exclusively outside this winter and am offering reduced rates as this is my first year doing photography in Chicago.

Check out the gallery below or check out my social media to see more of my work!

Services

Chicago Family Photography

Family portraits will always be a classic. I like to mix it up with some traditional poses, and then get everyone to forget the camera is there and just be themselves and capture those true, authentic moments. When you contact me about your family portraits, we can discuss the type of backdrop you prefer and then capture some amazing memories!

Chicago Elopement and Engagements

The intimacy of a small elopement ceremony or of an engagement shoot where it's just the two of you can't be beat. I've shot over 500 elopements, weddings and engagements in my career and each and every one is different. With the beautiful scenery around Chicago, we can capture some amazing moments of the two of you!
