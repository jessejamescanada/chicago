Who I am

I have been photographing weddings, elopements, family portraits, maternity and proposals for over 13 years now. Originally from Niagara Falls, Canada, I moved to Beaufort, SC recently! I love working with couples and families from all over the Beaufort area. There are so many beautiful backdrops around the low country area for your photography needs. Please take a look at the gallery below. If you want to see additional photos from recent shoots, give my Instagram or Facebook a follow!


***Over the past few years I have shifted my focus to smaller weddings & elopements, which due to Covid-19 that looks to be the future of weddings for the forseeable future. Please contact me to discuss your wedding photography needs further.***
