About Me

Hello there! My name is Jesse. I have been photographing family portraits, weddings, elopements, maternity and proposals for over 13 years now. Originally from Niagara Falls, Canada, I moved to Beaufort, SC recently!

What type of photography do I do?

Although my main focus is on photographing families, portraits and small weddings in the Beaufort and surrounding areas, I do occasionally photograph larger weddings as well.

Do I only take photos in Beaufort, SC?

I will shoot anywhere! I love working with couples and families from all over the Beaufort, Hilton Head, Charleston and surrounding areas, but I am always willing to travel as well. The nice thing about being a photographer in Beaufort...or all of the the low country really... is that there are so many beautiful backdrops to choose from!

Whats my favourite thing to photograph?

Honestly anything. I love photography. I'm very lucky that photography is my business but also my main hobby. For client work I love capturing family portraits, the intamacy of an elopement, the creativity that an engagement offers, or helping someone to look and feel their best during a portrait session. For fun I love to capture landscapes, wildlife, my dogs and space photos!

Whats my style?

My style is a mix of traditional along with a more modern and candid flare. I do give direction and I usually crack a lot of corny jokes to get whomever is in front of my camera a bit more relaxed

Whats included in my photography packages?

All of my photography packages come with high resolution images and an online gallery just for you!
