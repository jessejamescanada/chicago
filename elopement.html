Beaufort Elopements & Engagements

I love the intimacy of a small wedding, elopement or engagement shoot. The couples get to kick back and relax with their closest family and friends and we can focus more on the love you two share.


I find with elopements we can be a little more creative and get more photos of the two of you (and any guests) as the day is more relaxed.


Plus with the gorgeous backdrops that Beaufort, Charleston and Hilton Head offer, we have so many locations to choose from for your wedding, elopement or engagement photos!


My elopement & engagement packages start at $450. They include the fully retouched images in full resolution plus an online gallery.


I do offer a very limited amount of full wedding day packages (6 or 8 hour coverage).


Please contact me to discuss your elopement or engagement photo shoot!
